The start of World War One

Because of this, Serbia was declared war on by Austria-Hungary, beginning the first world war. Luxembourg and Belgium were invaded by Germany while Alsace was invaded by France. Forces from Great Britain arrive in France. There were many nations allied against Germany, and the allied forces began to increase.
